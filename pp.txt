Privacy Policy – DBUCalculator

Last Updated: 17/08/2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Bot, we may collect limited information such as:
    Discord user IDs (to identify users when saving settings or stats)
    Optional saved data (e.g., game rebirth stats, calculation preferences)

We do not collect:
    Private messages
    Sensitive personal information
    General chat content outside of bot commands

2. How We Use the Information

To perform calculations and respond to your commands
To store optional preferences/statistics for later use
To prevent misuse or abuse of the Bot

3. Data Storage & Retention

Only minimal data necessary for the Bot’s functionality is stored.
Stored data may include game-related numbers (e.g., rebirth counts) that you or your server choose to save.
Data is kept until it is no longer needed or until you request its removal.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
Data may only be shared if required by law or to comply with Discord’s terms.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your stored data at any time by contacting us.
Removing the Bot from your server does not automatically delete stored data — please contact us if you want all data removed.
